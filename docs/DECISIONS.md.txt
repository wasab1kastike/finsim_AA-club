# Decisions (ADRs)

## ADR-0001: Engine & Language
- **Decision**: Godot 4.x (GDScript)
- **Date**: 2025-09-09
- **Why**: Small team fit, easy exports, good scene graph for lanes
- **Alternatives**: Unity, Phaser/TS (deferred)

## ADR-0002: Branching model
- **Decision**: Trunk-based, short-lived feature branches
- **Examples**: feat/idle-tick, feat/lanes
- **Policy**: PRs into main, squash & merge

## ADR-0003: Commit style
